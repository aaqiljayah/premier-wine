📋 Terms & Conditions

1. Age Restriction

You must be aged 18 or over to purchase alcohol from Stuarts Premier Daily. By placing an order you confirm you meet this requirement. We operate Challenge 25 and may request ID upon delivery. We reserve the right to refuse delivery if we have reason to believe you are under 18.

2. Orders & Payment

All orders are subject to availability. Payment is by cash on delivery only. The exact amount shown at checkout must be ready when your order arrives. We cannot process orders where payment is unavailable on delivery.

3. Delivery

We deliver to local areas around Stowmarket, Suffolk. Delivery times are estimates only and may vary. We will contact you on the phone number provided to confirm your delivery slot. Delivery is to the address provided at checkout.

4. Cancellations

Orders may be cancelled by calling us on 01449 612259 before dispatch. Once an order has been dispatched for delivery we are unable to accept cancellations.

5. Prices & Availability

All prices shown include VAT at the current rate. Prices and product availability are subject to change without notice. In the event a product becomes unavailable after your order is placed, we will contact you to offer a suitable alternative or a full refund.

6. Responsible Drinking

Stuarts Premier Daily promotes responsible alcohol consumption. We will not knowingly sell alcohol to anyone who appears to be intoxicated or to anyone purchasing on behalf of a person under 18.

7. Privacy & Cookies

We collect your name, email, phone number and delivery address solely to process and fulfil your order. This information is stored locally in your browser and is not shared with third parties. We use essential cookies only, required for the shopping cart and session preferences to function.
